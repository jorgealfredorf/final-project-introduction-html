LF Etiqueta

Teaching core social and profesional etiquette principles.

At LF Etiqueta, we focus on cultivating essential social and professional etiquette skills that empower individuals to navigate various settings with confidence and respect. Our curriculum is designed to instill core values of courtesy, respect, and professionalism, while also fostering a deeper understanding of cultural nuances in both Dominican and international contexts.

Core Skills

  • Urbanity and social behavior: Understanding basic rules of courtesy and respectful interaction.
  • Good manners: Applying these principles in various social situations.
  • Dining etiquette: Proper use of table settings, cutlery, and glassware.
  • Seating and hosting protocol: Knowing how to sit, serve, and interact at formal meals.
  • Conversation etiquette: Balancing dialogue at the table with tact and respect.
  • Professional presence: Projecting confidence and poise in professional settings.

Supporting skills

  • Time management in social events: Respecting schedules and flow of meals.
  • Cultural awareness: Adapting etiquette to Dominican and international contexts.
  • Team interaction: Strengthening group cohesion through shared standards of conduct.
  • Attention to detail: Recognizing how small gestures (greetings, posture, tone) affect perception.
